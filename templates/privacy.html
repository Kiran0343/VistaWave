Privacy Policy

This page explains what information vistawave collects through the website, how it is used, and how to contact us with privacy questions.

Effective date: April 4, 2026

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly through contact forms, assessment requests, and job application forms. This may include your name, business contact details, company information, project details, and uploaded resume files.

How We Use Information

We use submitted information to respond to inquiries, evaluate consulting opportunities, review job applications, and communicate with you about the request you initiated.

Resume and File Uploads

Resume files submitted through the careers form are stored for recruiting review and internal evaluation. Access is limited to people involved in reviewing applications.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us operate the website, send email notifications, or store files securely.

Retention

We retain inquiry and application information for as long as reasonably necessary to respond, evaluate opportunities, maintain records, and meet legal or operational obligations.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect information submitted through the website. No internet transmission or storage system is guaranteed to be fully secure.

Your Choices

If you want to request access, correction, or deletion of information you submitted through the website, contact us using the email below.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to recruiter@vistawavepro.com.
