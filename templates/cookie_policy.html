Cookie Policy

This page explains how Vistawave uses cookies and related technologies to improve website performance, measure engagement, and support core functionality.

Effective date: April 4, 2026

What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites recognize repeat visits, remember preferences, and understand how pages are used.

How We Use Cookies

Vistawave may use cookies or similar technologies to support site performance, improve user experience, maintain security, and understand which content or conversion paths are most useful.

Types of Cookies

The site may use essential cookies for basic functionality, analytics cookies to understand traffic and interaction patterns, and service-related cookies connected to tools used for chat, monitoring, or embedded services.

Third-Party Services

Some website features may rely on third-party services that place their own cookies or use similar storage technologies. Those providers manage their own policies and controls.

Your Choices

Most browsers allow you to manage, block, or delete cookies through browser settings. Restricting cookies may affect how certain parts of the website function.
